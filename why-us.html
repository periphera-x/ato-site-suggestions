Core differentiator

AI-native training that most agencies can't offer

Before placement: Every assistant completes our structured AI training program: 5 sessions covering the AI landscape, prompting skills, tool fluency across multiple platforms, and applying AI to real work tasks.

After placement: Ongoing coaching during check-ins, group learning sessions, and new tools and workflows as they emerge. We set up their Cowork environment and install workflow-specific skills.

As long as the membership is active, we're offering more avenues for your assistant to grow.

Transparent pricing

Your assistant gets paid what they're worth

Most agencies charge $2,500–3,000/mo and pay assistants $700–800. You never see the split.

With ATO: $500/mo goes to ATO for recruiting, training, and support. $1,200–2,000/mo goes directly to your assistant at market rate. Every dollar accounted for.

Your total investment ($1,700–2,300/mo) is comparable to what other agencies may charge except your assistant earns significantly more. Higher pay means better candidates, better retention, and a stronger partnership.

Talent quality

Proactive, high-quality talent

Every candidate goes through a multi-stage vetting process: initial interview, tiered assessment task, and final interview. By the time we propose someone, we're already confident.

We measure every assistant against our Contributor Growth Curve. Our goal is Level 4+ for every placement.

With AI training on top of that, your assistant is ahead of the curve.

Personal support

No noise. Just people. Direct access.

You work directly with our leadership team. We're a small, tight-knit group and we stay involved long after the placement.

Month 1: Weekly check-ins.
Months 2–3: Bi-monthly.
After 90 days: As-needed.
Prefer async? No problem, we run the same cadence over your preferred platform.
