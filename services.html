Our process

A simple process for busy clients

We despise having to jump through hoops. Our process was built to prioritize realized value over perceived value.

1

Leading with understanding

After our intro call, you'll complete a quick 5–10 minute form on your work style, preferences, and priorities. We use these insights to craft a personalized "User Manual" that helps your future assistant understand exactly how to work best with you.

2

Finding your ideal candidate

We tap into our network, vet candidates, and propose 2–4 people who fit your criteria. You'll get their resumes, key highlights, and pre-negotiated rates. Run a 30-minute screening call to confirm the fit. Pick your favorite or give feedback and we'll find more.

3

Kickoff call & start

On your assistant's first day, we host a 30-minute kickoff call to confirm roles, responsibilities, and next steps. Your assistant arrives already AI-trained through our foundations program. After that, you're off and running.

4

Ongoing support for 90+ days

Weekly check-ins for the first 4 weeks. Bi-monthly for months 2–3. After 90 days: as-needed. Async option always available. AI training and coaching continue for the life of the membership.
